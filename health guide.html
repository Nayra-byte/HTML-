💪 Why Your Body Needs Exercise

Your body is a Supercar — the more you use it, the stronger it gets! Kids who move for 60 minutes every day are happier, sleep better, and do better at school.

Dancing, Swimming, Basketball, Yoga — anythingg that gets your heart beating faster counts. Keep moving and having fun!

☀ Quick health tips

  • Eat Fruits and Veggies
  • Drink 8 glasses of water daily
  • Sleep 9-11hrs
  • Move your body for 60 mins
